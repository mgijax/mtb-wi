Tumor Frequency (%)
The percentage of mice in a given study that develop a specific type of tumor.

Note: Observations of "zero percent frequency" are recorded in the Mouse Tumor Biology Database.

Often, subjective descriptions of tumor frequency are reported rather than exact proportions or percentages. We have converted non-exact text descriptions of tumor frequency into the following numerical categories for database searching:

Numerical equivalencies for text descriptions of tumor frequency

Frequency Description  Numerical Equivalent 
very high > 80%
high > 50%
moderate > 30%
low < 20%
very low <10%
sporadic < 1%
observed > 0.1%
