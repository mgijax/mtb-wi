Tumor Frequency Range
The lowest and highest reported frequencies for the given tumor. This field provides a digest of all papers reporting tumors that arose in the same strain under identical conditions.

The reported frequencies may have arisen at various ages and/or under various breeding conditions.

Frequencies are broken down by sex of the mice:
  • Female
  • Male
  • Mixed= both male and female mice, but individual incidences were not provided
  • Unspecified=sex of mice was not specified
